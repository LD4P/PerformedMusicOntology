PMO Disc Cutting Method Vocabularyc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: http://performedmusicontology.org/ontologies/vocabularies/disc_cutting/

The PMO Disc Cutting Method Vocabulary is a controlled vocabulary for how a stylus cuts a phonograph record during the recording process. Terms are derived from the MARC21 bibliographic format 007/?? codes. It was developed for the Performed Music Ontology, but may be used with other ontologies.
