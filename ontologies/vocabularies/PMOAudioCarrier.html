PMO Audio Carrier Vocabulary

http://performedmusicontology.org/ontologies/vocabularies/audio_carrier/

The PMO Music Tonal Center Vocabulary is a controlled vocabulary for specific and colloquial terms for carriers of audio recordings.

CD Audioc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: http://performedmusicontology.org/ontologies/vocabularies/audio_carrier/cd_audio

Definition: Optical discs that use the standard compact disc format for audio. They are 12 cm (4 3/4 in.) in diameter, and encoded as 2-channel signed 16-bit Linear PCM sampled at 44,100 Hz.

Current version: NEW 2018-07-01

Has broader terms
http://rdaregistry.info/termList/RDACarrierType/1004

DATc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: http://performedmusicontology.org/ontologies/vocabularies/audio_carrier/dat

Definition: Sony's proprietary Digital Audio Tape format, characterized by magnetic media enclosed in a protective shell, with the dimensions of 8 x 6 cm, 4 mm tape or 2 7/8 x 2 1/8 in., 1/8 in. tape.

Current version: NEW 2018-07-01

Has broader terms
http://rdaregistry.info/termList/RDACarrierType/1007

45 recordingc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: http://performedmusicontology.org/ontologies/vocabularies/audio_carrier/disc_45

Definition: Colloquial term representing 7" diameter vinyl audio discs with a playing speed of 45 rpm. The term does not necessarily describe the carrier accurately.
