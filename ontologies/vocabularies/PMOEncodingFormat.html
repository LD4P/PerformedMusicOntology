PMO Encoding Format Vocabulary

http://performedmusicontology.org/ontologies/vocabularies/encoding_format/

The PMO Encoding Format Vocabulary is a controlled vocabulary for stating encoding formats of audio recordings. It was developed for the Performed Music Ontology, but may be used with other ontologies.
