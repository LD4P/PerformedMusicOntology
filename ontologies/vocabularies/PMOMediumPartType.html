PMO Medium Part Type Vocabulary

http://performedmusicontology.org/ontologies/vocabularies/medium_part_type/

The PMO Music Tonal Center Vocabulary is a controlled vocabulary for stating specialized instructions about a medium of performance (e.g., obbligato, offstage). It was developed for the Performed Music Ontology, but may be used with other ontologies.
