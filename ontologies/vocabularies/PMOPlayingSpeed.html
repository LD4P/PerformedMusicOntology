PMO Playing Speed Vocabulary

http://performedmusicontology.org/ontologies/vocabularies/playing_speed/

The PMO Playing Speed Vocabulary is a controlled vocabulary for playing speeds of audio recordings. Terms are derived from the MARC21 bibliographic format 007/?? codes. It was developed for the Performed Music Ontology, but may be used with other ontologies.

IRI: http://performedmusicontology.org/ontologies/vocabularies/playing_speed/fpm_12

Definition: Playing speed of 12 feet per minute x 10.

Note: Used for piano and organ rolls.
